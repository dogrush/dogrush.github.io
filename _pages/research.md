<div class="section-block">
  <h2 class="section-title">Research Overview</h2>
  <p style="font-size: 1.05rem; line-height: 1.8; color: #555;">
    Our research group (CI-Lab) at Beihang University is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of intelligent computing through innovative research and development. We explore a wide range of topics including machine learning systems, large language model (LLM) infrastructure, emerging computer architectures, and systems for embodied intelligence.
  </p>
</div>
