Our research group (CI-Lab) focuses on advancing the frontiers of intelligent computing through innovative research and development. Below are our main research interests.

<div class="research-direction">
  <img src="../images/research-arch.svg" alt="Intelligent Computing Systems and Architectures" class="research-img">
  <div class="research-info">
    <h3>Intelligent Computing Systems and Architectures</h3>
    <p>We explore novel computer architectures designed for intelligent computing workloads, including accelerator design, hardware-software co-optimization, and domain-specific architectures for AI applications.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="research-direction">
  <img src="../images/research-mlsys.svg" alt="ML/LLM Systems and Infrastructure" class="research-img">
  <div class="research-info">
    <h3>ML/LLM Systems and Infrastructure</h3>
    <p>We research efficient systems and infrastructure for machine learning and large language models, covering training/inference optimization, distributed systems, and resource management for large-scale AI workloads.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="research-direction">
  <img src="../images/research-embodied.svg" alt="Systems for Embodied Intelligence" class="research-img">
  <div class="research-info">
    <h3>Systems for Embodied Intelligence</h3>
    <p>We investigate computing systems that bridge AI with the physical world, including robotics systems, real-time perception-processing pipelines, and edge-cloud collaborative architectures for embodied AI applications.</p>
  </div>
</div>
